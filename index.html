旅游行程预排表Travel Itinerary Pre Schedule
备注

  • 行程的第一部分从后贝加尔斯克到伊尔库茨克需要11天时间。深入游览贝加尔湖，去贝加尔湖看气泡冰。我们还计划在俄罗斯狩猎场狩猎。
  • 行程的第二部分将从伊尔库茨克穿越西伯利亚，历时14天到达莫斯科。
  • 这次行程的第三部分将从莫斯科到圣彼得堡，耗时5天。
  • 这次行程的第四段是从圣彼得堡乘飞机到摩尔曼斯克，在那里停留一段时间，然后通过莫斯科转机返回中国。
  • 在中国申请90天的个人旅行签证。如果签证时间较长，我们会在摩尔曼斯克能拍到极光的小镇多停留一段时间。
  • 理想的旅行时间是11月和12月，在那里你可以捕捉到美丽的极光。以下计划来源于互联网。请向当地专业旅行社寻求帮助。
  • The first section of this itinerary will take 11 days from Post Baikalsk to Irkutsk. Deep tour around Lake Baikal, go to Lake Baikal to see bubble ice. We also plan to hunt in Russian hunting grounds.
  • The second section of this itinerary will take 14 days from Irkutsk to Moscow, crossing Siberia.
  • The third segment of this itinerary will take 5 days from Moscow to St. Petersburg.
  • The fourth segment of this itinerary is to take a plane from St. Petersburg to Murmansk, stop there for a period of time, and then transfer back to China through Moscow.
  • Apply for a 90 day personal travel visa in China. If the visa period is longer, we will stay in a small town in Murmansk that can capture the aurora for an additional period of time.
  • The ideal travel time is November and December, where you can capture beautiful auroras. The following plans are sourced from the internet. Please ask a professional local travel agency to help us arrange them.
